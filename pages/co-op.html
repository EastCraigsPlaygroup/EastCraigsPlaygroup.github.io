We are Co-Op Community Funded

We are delighted to announce that we have been accepted as part of the Co-Op Local Community Fund! This means that shopping at the Co-Op at Bughtlin Market is an easy way to raise funds for our playgroup.

When you become a member with the Co-Op 1% of what you spend on selected own-brand products and services goes to the Co-op Local Community Fund.

If you would like to support East Craigs Playgroup please become a member at the Co-Op, go online to register then choose East Craigs Playgroup as your local cause and get shopping!

Please Remember to tell anyone you know to register support for East Craigs Playgroup!
